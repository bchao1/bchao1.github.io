Brian Chao

I'm a 3rd year PhD student at Stanford University working in the Stanford Computational Imaging Lab, advised by Prof. Gordon Wetzstein. I'm grateful to be supported by the NSF GRFP and the Stanford Graduate Fellowship.

My research focuses on the integration of physics and ML techniques (neural rendering, generative models, etc) for novel imaging and display system design to enable new capabilities in AR/VR and scene reconstruction.

I've interned at Meta Reality Labs, where I worked on neural rendering for 3D reconstruction. I've also served as a reviewer for SIGGRAPH, NeuRIPS, ISMAR, TIP, ICIP, and ICASSP.
